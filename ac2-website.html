Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?
+
Most web projects take 4–12 weeks depending on scope. We provide a detailed timeline after our discovery call so you always know what to expect.
What technologies do you specialize in?
+
Our core stack includes React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, AWS, and various AI/ML frameworks. We adapt to your existing tech stack when needed.
Do you offer post-launch support?
+
Yes — all plans include dedicated support. Growth and Enterprise clients get priority response times and proactive monitoring with regular performance reports.
Can you work with our in-house team?
+
Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with client teams as an extension of your existing engineering or design department, adapting to your workflow and tools.
How do you handle intellectual property?
+
All code and design assets are fully transferred to you upon project completion and final payment. You own everything we build for you — 100%.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Yes! We serve clients globally. Our async-first communication style and flexible scheduling make cross-timezone collaboration seamless and effective.
